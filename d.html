How to Contribute

Contribution Guidelines

We welcome contributions from developers of all skill levels. Before submitting a resource, please ensure it meets the following criteria:

  • Quality: Resources should be well-maintained, up-to-date, and valuable to developers.
  • Relevance: Resources should be directly related to software development, programming, or adjacent fields.
  • Free Access: Preferred resources are free to use, though we do include some premium tools that offer significant value.
  • No Affiliate Links: Please do not submit resources with affiliate links or promotional content.

Your submission will be reviewed by our team. We may edit for clarity or categorization before publishing.

Contribution Process

1. Check for Duplicates: Before submitting, please check if the resource already exists in our collection.

2. Fill the Form: Complete the contribution form with accurate information about the resource.

3. Review: Our team will review your submission within 48 hours.

4. Publication: Once approved, your resource will be added to the appropriate category.

5. Recognition: Contributors are credited on our site (if you provide your username).

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my submission to be reviewed?

We typically review submissions within 48 hours. During busy periods, it might take up to a week. You will receive an email notification once your submission has been processed.

Can I submit my own project or website?

Yes, you can submit your own project as long as it provides genuine value to developers and meets our quality guidelines. Self-promotion without substantial value is not accepted.

What if I suggested a resource but it's not added?

We receive many submissions and need to maintain quality and relevance. If your resource wasn't added, it might be because it didn't meet our guidelines, was a duplicate, or we felt it wasn't the right fit for our audience.

Can I update a resource I previously submitted?

Yes, if you need to update a resource you submitted, please contact us at contribute@devresourceshub.com with the details of your original submission and the updates needed.
